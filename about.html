About

Oakland born, Oakland raised, FU$ion, a kid Hip-Hop artist, is known for his meaningful lyrics. FU$ion’s music is the perfect balance of New era rhymes. He has proven he is capable of growing and sustaining a loyal fan base. The culture of FU$ion’s brand revolves solely around his fans and their everyday struggles and successes. FU$ion consists of fans of all ages, ethnicities, and interests. FU$ion has many goals he has set out to achieve, but his main goal is to continue to inspire others to change the world. Beginning his Rap career in early High School helped FU$ion become the emerging artist he is today. inspires kids and adults across the globe to keep their head up.
